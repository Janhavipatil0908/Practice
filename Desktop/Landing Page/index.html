A smart solution to everyday shopping, seamlessly integrated into one app!
ShopEase simplifies organizing shopping lists with real-time sharing, inventory tracking, and receipt attachments.

Get started with ShopEase :
Step 1: Sign up with your mobile number - Download & install the app from Play Store or App Store. - Provide your mobile number. - Enter the OTP to successfully sign in to the app.
Step 2: Create the shopping list - Navigate to the checklist. - Add items using the scan option or the add option. - Link the items to a specific shop. - Check off the items as you purchase them. - Complete your shopping.
Step 3: Upload the receipt - Upload the receipt when the purchase is completed or do it later from the history. - Click on the receipt image placeholder. - Upload from the gallery or take a picture. - Successfully attach the invoice.
